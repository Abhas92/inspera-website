The Problem

Your firm is leaking 15–20% of revenue

Disconnection hurts. Here's how it shows up every day.

Profitability is a guess

You finish projects without knowing if you made or lost money. Margins only show up when the accountant reconciles — months later.

Milestones slip, billing delays

No alerts when deliverables are overdue. Invoices go out late. Cash flow suffers silently.

Leads fall through the cracks

No system tracks inquiry to proposal to contract. Follow-ups happen on memory, not process.

Scattered across 4+ tools

Site updates in one app. Timesheets in another. Accounts somewhere else. Approvals over email. Nothing connects.

No single view of your firm

No dashboard connecting projects, people, and payments. Decisions made on gut feel, not data.

Month-end HR chaos

Attendance, payroll, and project costing reconciled only at month-end. Budget surprises and overtime disputes.

The Platform

Everything your firm needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Six integrated modules that replace fragmented tools with one connected platform.

Projects & Contracts

Milestone-driven project management with a hierarchical planner — Contract, Milestone, Deliverable, Task. Track every phase from contract signing to final handover.

Real-time weighted completion tracking

Site Updates & Approvals

Real-time field updates with photo logs, material tracking, and multi-level approval chains. Keep the office and site in sync without chasing messages.

Bridge the site-to-office gap instantly

HR, Payroll & Timesheets

Leave management, expense claims, attendance, payroll processing, and project-linked timesheets — all in one place. No more switching between separate systems.

Timesheets linked directly to project milestones

Accounts & Finance

Sales, procurement, GST-ready invoicing, payments, and journal entries — with automatic linkage to project milestones. Know your margins before the project ends.

Milestone-to-invoice auto-linkage

CRM for Design Firms

Track leads with fields built for your industry — land area, design style, project classification, budget range. Follow every opportunity from first inquiry to project kickoff.

Industry-specific lead qualification

Two Views, One Platform

Designers see projects, timelines, and deliverables. Admins see finances, compliance, and operations. Same data, tailored experiences — everyone gets exactly what they need.

One platform, zero compromises
